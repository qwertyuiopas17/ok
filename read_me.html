Welcome to SAHARA SAATHI - SIH 2025 Project

A very warm welcome to our SIH 2025 project (MVP). Alongside the frontend, we have also created a backend hosted on Render.com. We have integrated AI for various purposes that you'll discover after logging in.

However, please note that many features are still in development and will be rolled out in the future, especially for the admin, saathi, and store panels.

How to Register

For Patients

Simply register and login as you would on any other website. Fill in your details and create an account to access patient features.

For Other Roles (Doctor, Store, Saathi, Admin)

Since access for these roles will be provided by admin/authorities, we have added demo profiles with working backend functionality for testing purposes.
